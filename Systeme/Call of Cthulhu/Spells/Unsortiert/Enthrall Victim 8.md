Enthrall Victim 8 6
• Cost:  2 magic points; 1D6 Sanity points
•
 Cast
ing time: 1 round
The caster must be able to speak calmly with the target 
before the spell can take effect. After a round or so of 
talking, an opposed POW roll is made between the caster 
and target:  if the caster is successful, the target stands 
struck, numb and dumb, until relieved from the trance by 
physical assault or some similarly shocking event. 
Alternative names: Strike Dumb, The Silver Beguiling 
Tongue.
