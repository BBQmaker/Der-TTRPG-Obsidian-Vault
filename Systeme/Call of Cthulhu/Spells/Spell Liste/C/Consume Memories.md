Consume Memories O
• Cost:  10 magic points; 1D10 Sanity points
•
 Cast
ing time: 1D6 rounds
Allows the caster to absorb the memories and skills of 
whoever’s (fresh) brain the caster consumes. For twenty-
four hours after the brain is consumed, the caster may 
use any and all skills and knowledge the victim possessed. 
At the end of this time, the caster loses all the skills and 
knowledge gained. 
Deeper magic: the memories of the victim can be permanently 
absorbed if the caster sacrifices 20 POW . 
Alternative names: Wisdom of the Forbidden Fruit, Imbibe 
thy Enemies’ Will.
